# Action Library Reference

Standard action types and when to use them.

## Meeting Actions

### Schedule Discovery Meeting

**When:** Early stage, need to understand needs
**Likelihood Factors:** Champion engagement, availability
**Effort:** Low (15-25)
**Champion Value:** Medium (50-70)

### Schedule Demo

**When:** Prospect ready to see solution
**Likelihood Factors:** Interest confirmed, stakeholders available
**Effort:** Medium (30-50)
**Champion Value:** High (70-80)

### Schedule Technical Review

**When:** Technical evaluation phase
**Likelihood Factors:** IT/Engineering engaged, requirements clear
**Effort:** Medium (40-60)
**Champion Value:** Medium (50-70)

### Schedule Executive Meeting

**When:** Need executive sponsorship or unstick deal
**Likelihood Factors:** Access available, executive interested
**Effort:** Medium (30-50)
**Champion Value:** High (60-90)

### Schedule Proposal Review

**When:** Proposal sent, need to review
**Likelihood Factors:** Proposal received, stakeholders ready
**Effort:** Low (20-30)
**Champion Value:** High (70-80)

### Engage Through Champion

**Likelihood Factors:** Champion willing, stakeholder receptive

## Proposal/Close Actions

### Send Proposal

**When:** Discovery complete, ready to quote
**Likelihood Factors:** Scope clear, budget known, stakeholders aligned
**Effort:** Medium (30-50)
**Champion Value:** High (65-80)

### Negotiate Terms

**When:** Proposal sent, terms need work
**Likelihood Factors:** Decision-maker engaged, terms flexible
**Effort:** Medium (30-50)
**Champion Value:** Medium (50-70)

### Send Contract

**When:** Terms agreed, ready for legal
**Likelihood Factors:** All approvals in place, terms finalized
**Effort:** Low (15-30)
**Champion Value:** High (60-75)

### Follow Up on Signature

**When:** Contract sent, awaiting signature
**Likelihood Factors:** Everything approved, just needs signature
**Effort:** Very Low (5-15)
**Champion Value:** Medium (40-60)

## Follow-Up Actions

### Check-In After Meeting

**When:** Meeting held, want to maintain momentum
**Likelihood Factors:** Meeting went well, action items exist
**Effort:** Very Low (5-15)
**Champion Value:** Medium (40-60)

### Follow Up on Outstanding Item

**When:** Waiting on something from prospect
**Likelihood Factors:** Item was promised, time has passed
**Effort:** Very Low (5-15)
**Champion Value:** Low (30-50)

### Reactivate Stalled Deal

**When:** Deal has gone quiet
**Likelihood Factors:** Relationship still exists, situation may have changed
**Effort:** Low (15-30)
**Champion Value:** Medium (40-60)

| Lead | Research, outreach, introduction request |
| Target | Discovery meeting, case study, stakeholder mapping |
| Discovery | Demo, technical review, champion enablement |
| Contracting | Proposal, negotiation, executive alignment |
| Production | Pilot, implementation planning, reference call |

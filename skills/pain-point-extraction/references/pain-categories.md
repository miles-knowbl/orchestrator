# Pain Point Categories Reference

Standard taxonomy for categorizing prospect pain points.

## Category Definitions

### Volume

**Definition:** Capacity and scale issues — too much work, not enough resources.

**Keywords:**
- "drowning", "overwhelmed", "swamped"
- "too many", "can't keep up", "backlog"
- "scaling issues", "capacity", "volume"

**Examples:**
- "We're drowning in support tickets"
- "Can't hire fast enough to keep up with growth"
- "Backlog of 500+ requests"

**AI Solution Fit:** High — automation excels at volume problems

---

### Cost

**Definition:** Cost pressure — spending too much, need to reduce costs.

**Keywords:**
- "expensive", "cost", "budget"
- "spending too much", "ROI", "cost savings"
- "reduce costs", "efficiency", "headcount"

**Examples:**
- "Support costs growing faster than revenue"
- "Need to reduce cost per ticket"
- "Can't justify hiring more people"

**AI Solution Fit:** High — automation reduces per-unit costs

---

### Quality

**Definition:** Quality problems — errors, mistakes, inconsistency.

**Keywords:**
- "errors", "mistakes", "wrong"
- "complaints", "inconsistent", "accuracy"
- "quality issues", "rework", "defects"

**Examples:**
- "Too many errors in responses"
- "Inconsistent answers to same question"
- "Quality varies by agent"

**AI Solution Fit:** High — AI provides consistent quality

---

### Speed

**Definition:** Time and efficiency issues — too slow, customers waiting.

**Keywords:**
- "slow", "takes too long", "waiting"
- "delays", "response time", "SLA"
- "fast enough", "speed", "turnaround"

**Examples:**
- "Response times are too slow"
- "Customers waiting 24+ hours"
- "Missing SLA targets"

**AI Solution Fit:** High — AI provides instant responses

---

### Compliance

**Definition:** Regulatory and compliance requirements.

**Keywords:**
- "audit", "compliance", "regulation"
- "SOC 2", "GDPR", "HIPAA", "PCI"
- "regulatory", "requirements", "certified"

**Examples:**
- "Need SOC 2 for enterprise deals"
- "GDPR data residency requirements"
- "Audit trail for all interactions"

**AI Solution Fit:** Medium — depends on compliance capabilities

---

### Security

**Definition:** Security concerns — data protection, breaches, vulnerabilities.

**Keywords:**
- "breach", "vulnerability", "secure"
- "protect", "security", "attack"
- "data protection", "encryption", "access control"

**Examples:**
- "Had a security incident last year"
- "Customer data must stay in our environment"
- "Need enterprise-grade security"

**AI Solution Fit:** Medium — security is table stakes, not differentiator

---

### Experience

**Definition:** Customer or user experience problems.

**Keywords:**
- "frustrated", "complaints", "satisfaction"
- "NPS", "CSAT", "experience"
- "unhappy", "churning", "feedback"

**Examples:**
- "NPS dropped 15 points"
- "Customers frustrated with wait times"
- "Getting complaints on social media"

**AI Solution Fit:** High — better CX is core AI benefit

---

### Talent

**Definition:** People and hiring issues.

**Keywords:**
- "can't hire", "training", "turnover"
- "expertise", "skills gap", "recruiting"
- "retention", "burnout", "staffing"

**Examples:**
- "Can't hire support agents fast enough"
- "High turnover, always training new people"
- "Hard to find people with right skills"

**AI Solution Fit:** High — AI augments existing team

---

### Legacy

**Definition:** Old system and technical debt problems.

**Keywords:**
- "outdated", "legacy", "old system"
- "technical debt", "ancient", "deprecated"
- "replacing", "migration", "upgrade"

**Examples:**
- "Running on 10-year-old platform"
- "Technical debt slowing us down"
- "Need to modernize"

**AI Solution Fit:** Medium — AI can layer on top but doesn't fix legacy

---

### Integration

**Definition:** Connection and data flow issues.

**Keywords:**
- "doesn't talk to", "manual", "disconnected"
- "silos", "integration", "API"
- "copy/paste", "export/import", "sync"

**Examples:**
- "Systems don't talk to each other"
- "Manual data entry between tools"
- "No single view of customer"

**AI Solution Fit:** Medium — depends on integration capabilities

---

## Multi-Category Pain

Some pain points span multiple categories:

| Combined Pain | Categories |
|---------------|------------|
| "Slow responses costing us customers" | speed + experience + cost |
| "Can't hire fast enough to handle volume" | talent + volume |
| "Manual processes causing errors" | integration + quality |
| "Old system can't scale" | legacy + volume |

When pain spans categories, pick the **primary** category (most emphasized) and note **secondary** categories.
